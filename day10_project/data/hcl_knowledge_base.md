## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Visit https://portal.hcl.com/password-reset or call IT Help Desk at ext. 5000

### Q: What is the process for hardware requests?

A: Submit a ticket in ServiceNow under "Hardware Request" category. Approval required for devices over $1,500.

### Q: How do I access employee benefits?

A: Login to HR portal at https://hr.hcl.com with your employee ID and password.

### Q: What are the office hours?

A: Core hours are 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Flexible hours policy allows start time between 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM.

### Q: How do I report a security incident?

A: Email security@hcl.com immediately or call Security Operations Center at ext. 9999

### Q: What is the expense reimbursement policy?

A: Submit expenses in Concur within 30 days. Manager approval required. Receipts mandatory for expenses over $25.

## Software Development Guidelines

### Code Review Process

1. Create pull request
2. Minimum 2 approvals required
3. Automated tests must pass
4. Security scan clear
5. Merge after approval

### Deployment Windows

- Production: Tuesday/Thursday 10 PM - 2 AM
- Staging: Any time during business hours
- Emergency: With VP approval

### Security Requirements

- All APIs must use OAuth 2.0
- HTTPS only
- Input validation mandatory
- SQL injection prevention
- XSS protection required
